  4. April 2009

    G20 Leaders - London, United Kingdom

    "The era of banking secrecy is over"

  5. September 2009

    Exchange of Information on Request

    Global Forum Plenary - Mexico City, Mexico

    The Global Forum was established in its current form, to include all countries and jurisdictions committed to implement Exchange of Information on Request and agrees to carry out peer reviews to monitor progress.

    G20 Leaders - Pittsburgh, United States

    “We welcome the expansion of the Global Forum on Transparency and Exchange of Information, including the participation of developing countries, and welcome the agreement to deliver an effective program of peer reviews.”

    89 members

  6. September 2010

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance

    Global Forum Plenary - Singapore

    The Global Forum agrees to develop a technical assistance programme.

    Global Forum members: 94

  7. June 2011

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance

    Global Forum Plenary - Bermuda

    The Global Forum agrees to establish a platform for Competent Authorities to exchange best practices and enhance their relationships.

    Global Forum members: 104

  8. October 2011

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance

    Global Forum Plenary - Paris, France

    The Global Forum agrees to extend its mandate until the end of 2015.

    The Global Forum welcomes two pilot projects, funded by the UK Department for International Development (DFID), to assist Ghana and Kenya to build capacity and reinforce the legal infrastructure necessary for tax transparency and international co-operation.

    Impact to date

    €14 billion in additional revenue identified

    Half a million individuals have voluntarily disclosed hidden assets

    Global Forum members: 104

  9. May 2012

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance

    Impact to date

    First Competent Authorities meeting, Madrid, Spain

    Global Forum members: 115

  10. June 2012

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance

    G20 Leaders - Los Cabos, Mexico

    "We welcome the OECD report on the practice of automatic information exchange, where we will continue to lead by example in implementing this practice."

    Global Forum members: 115

  11. October 2012

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance

    Global Forum Plenary - Cape Town, South Africa

    The Global Forum agrees to review the EOIR Terms of Reference in the light of the experience gained and the evolution of the exchange of information environment.

    Global Forum members: 115

  12. September 2013

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance

    G20 Leaders - Saint Petersbourg, Russia

    "We urge all jurisdictions to address the Global Forum recommendations in particular those 14 that have not yet moved to Phase 2. We invite the Global Forum to draw on the work of the FATF with respect to beneficial ownership. We also ask the Global Forum to establish a mechanism to monitor and review the implementation of the new global standard on automatic exchange of information"

    Global Forum members: 120

  13. November 2013

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance

    Global Forum Plenary - Jakarta, Indonedia

    First 50 EOIR ratings adopted

    The Global Forum agreed that, in order to ensure a continuous monitoring of implementation of EOI on request, after completion of the current round of reviews, a further round of reviews should be conducted starting in 2016.

    The Global Forum established the AEOI Group to take forward to Global Forum’s work on AEOI.

    Global Forum members: 120

  14. February 2014

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance

    G20 Finance Ministers - Sydney, Australia

    "G20 Finance Ministers endorse the Standard for Automatic Exchange of Financial Account Information in Tax Matters developed by the OECD (the AEOI Standard)."

    Global Forum members: 123

  15. October 2014

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance

    Global Forum Plenary - Berlin, Germany

    Global Forum members endorse the AEOI standard and 89 members commit to start exchanges under the AEOI Standard in 2017 or 2018.

    51 jurisdictions participate in the first ever signing ceremony for the CRS MCAA for exchanges under the AEOI Standard.

    Launch of the Africa Initiative: a three-year project to raise awareness and build the tools to foster effective exchange of information led by African members and the Chair of the Global Forum with the collaboration of the Global Forum, ATAF, CREDAF, the OECD and the World Bank Group and the support of UK and France.

    Global Forum members: 123

  16. November 2014

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance

    G20 - Brisbane, Australia

    "To prevent cross-border tax evasion, we endorse the global Common Reporting Standard for the automatic exchange of tax information (AEOI) on a reciprocal basis. We will begin to exchange information automatically with each other and with other countries by 2017 or end-2018, subject to completing necessary legislative procedures. We welcome financial centres' commitments to do the same and call on all to join us."

    Impact to date

    €37 billion additional revenue identified

    Global Forum members: 123

  17. October 2015

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance

    Global Forum Plenary - Bridgetown, Barbados

    Global Forum adopts the revised Terms of Reference for EOIR, introducing a requirement that beneficial ownership information be available.

    Global Forum members: 129

  18. November 2015

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance

    G20 Leaders - Antalya, Turkey

    "We are progressing towards enhancing the transparency of our tax systems and we reaffirm our previous commitments to information exchange on-request as well as to automatic exchange of information by 2017 or end-2018. We invite other jurisdictions to join us. We support the efforts for strengthening developing economies' engagement in the international tax agenda."

    Impact to date

    €48 billion additional revenue identified

    Global Forum members: 129

  19. April 2016

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance

    News event

    Panama Papers

    Global Forum members: 137

  20. September 2016

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance

    G20 Leaders - Hangzhou, China

    "We also welcome the progress made on effective and widespread implementation of the internationally agreed standards on tax transparency and reiterate our call on all relevant countries including all financial centers and jurisdictions, which have not yet done so to commit without delay to implementing the standard of automatic exchange of information by 2018 at the latest and to sign and ratify the Multilateral Convention on Mutual Administrative Assistance in Tax Matters."

    Impact to date

    Almost € 55 billion in additional revenue identified

    Global Forum members: 137

  21. November 2016

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance

    Global Forum Plenary - Tbilisi, Georgia

    Global Forum members welcomed the finalisation of the first round of peer reviews on EOIR and agreed to take forward further work on beneficial ownership.

    Global Forum members: 137

  22. July 2017

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance

    G20 Leaders - Hamburg, Germany

    "We look forward to the first automatic exchange of financial account information under the Common Reporting Standard (CRS) in September 2017. We call on all relevant jurisdictions to begin exchanges by September 2018 at the latest."

    Impact to date

    € 85 billion in additional revenue identified

    Global Forum members: 147

  23. September 2017

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance + Automatic Exchange of Tax Information

    Impact to date

    First exchanges by around 50 “early adopters” under the AEOI Standard, using the Common Transmission System (CTS), developed by the OECD’s Forum on Tax Administration and managed by the Global Forum.

    Global Forum members: 147

  24. November 2017

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance + Automatic Exchange of Tax Information

    Global Forum Plenary - Yaoundé, Cameroon

    The Africa Initiative is renewed for a further 3 years with the support of additional donors (EU, Norway, Switzerland) and partners (WATAF, AU, AfDB).

    The Yaoundé Declaration is signed by the Ministers of Finance of Cameroon, Liberia, Uganda and Benin, calling for action by the African Union to take forward discussions on tax cooperation and illicit financial flows and their link to domestic resource mobilisation. It is now supported by 25 African Ministers of Finance.

    Publication of the 2017 AEOI Implementation report, setting out the implementation status of all committed jurisdictions, the first of its kind.

    Adoption of the Global Forum’s Plan of Action for Developing Countries’ Participation in AEOI.

    in the news

    Paradise Papers

    Global Forum members: 147

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance + Automatic Exchange of Tax Information

    Impact to date

    Global Forum members: 154

  26. September 2018

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance + Automatic Exchange of Tax Information

    Impact to date

    Global Forum members: 154

  27. October 2018

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance + Automatic Exchange of Tax Information

    in the news

    CumEx-Files

    Global Forum members: 154

  28. November 2018

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance + Automatic Exchange of Tax Information

    Global Forum Plenary - Punta del Este, Uruguay

    The Global Forum adopts the Terms of Reference for the AEOI Reviews, including in relation to its effectiveness in practice.

    The GF adopts a framework for post-exchange assessments of confidentiality for AEOI.

    Signing of the Punta del Este declaration on Strengthening Regional Efforts to Combat Tax Fraud and Corruption by Uruguay, Argentina, Panama and Paraguay (following the meeting, Ecuador, Colombia, Chile and Peru adhered to the Declaration). Paragraph 6 of the Declaration provides that signatory jurisdictions will further consider the possibility of wider use of the information exchanged for other law enforcement purposes.

    Publication of the 2018 AEOI Implementation Report

    Global Forum members: 154

  29. December 2018

    Exchange of Information on Request + Technical Assistance + Automatic Exchange of Tax Information

    G20 Leaders - Buenos Aires, Argentina

    "We welcome the commencement of the automatic exchange of financial account information and acknowledge the strengthened criteria developed by the OECD to identify jurisdictions that have not satisfactorily implemented the tax transparency standards."

    Impact to date

    Information on 47 million offshore accounts exchanged in 2018, with a total value of € 4.9 trillion

    Global Forum members: 154
